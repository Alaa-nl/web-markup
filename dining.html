Dining

De Pijp has the most restaurants in Amsterdam! Take even a short stroll around the darling little streets and you’ll come across terrace after terrace of hungry Amsterdammers.

So whether you’re temporarily settled in a hotel in de Pijp or are just happen to be walking through, you definitely won’t want to pass up the wonderful diversity of food you can find here! Below you can find our top spots to eat in the neighborhood.

  • 1- Alberto Pozzetto

    The Italian Restaurant – a hidden gem in Amsterdam! This cozy spot has been locals regular haunt for years , and it's worth visiting several times a week. You will find excellent coffee and a welcoming service that keeps you coming back. Their kitchen prepares mouthwatering sandwiches, hot meals, pies, and cakes using the finest Italian ingredients. If you're in Amsterdam, don't miss out on this delightful Italian dining experience.

    Form more information visit Alberto Pozzetto website.

    Address :Eerste van der Helststraat 57, 1073 AD Amsterdam

  • 2- Sonny Falafel & Vlaamse Frites

    Located in a great spot, this restaurant is your go-to place for a relaxed meal, especially if you're into hearty salads. But here's the real treat: they serve the most amazing falafel and fries, with a fantastic variety of delicious toppings to choose from. It's not just food; it's a flavor-packed adventure waiting for you! Whether you're a food enthusiast or just looking for a tasty bite, this place is where your taste buds will dance with delight.

    Form more information visit Sonny-Falafel website.

    Address: Eerste van der Helststraat 43, 1073 AC Amsterdam

  • 3- De Pizzakamer

    The best pizza place in Amsterdam where locals have pizza!

    Form more information visit De Pizzakamer website.

    Address: Tweede van der Helststraat 16, 1072 PD Amsterdam

  • 4- Kaasbar Amsterdam

    MAKE CHEESE NOT WAR If you are a cheese lover, this is the place you have to visit during your stay in Amsterdam! Unique experience, amazing choices and all so delicious, strongly recommend!

    Form more information visit Kaasbar Amsterdam website.

    Address: Ferdinand Bolstraat 10, 1072 LJ Amsterdam
