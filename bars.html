Bars

The neighborhood features many popular nighttime hangouts, including down-to-earth local pubs, cocktail lounges and trendy cafes. Keep reading to discover some of the best watering-holes in the neighborhood.

  • 1- GLOUGLOU2

    Glouglou is a hipster wine bar in the heart of De Pijp where you can enjoy the best wine in town and meet a lot of cool people.

    Form more information visit Glouglou website.

    Address: Tweede van der Helststraat 3, 1073 AE Amsterdam

  • 2- De Tulp

    Beautifully designed where you truly don't feel like you're in a big busy city, but rather somewhere tropical. Ideal after visiting the HEINEKEN EXPERIENCE only a minute down the road.

    Form more information visit De Tulp website.

    Address: Marie Heinekenplein 33, 1072 MH Amsterdam, Netherlands

  • 3- BARCA4

    Amazing mediterranean bar in oud De Pijp Amsterdam. It is a great place to stop for drinks and tapas while you are walking through the area

    Form more information visit Barca website.

    Address: Marie Heinekenplein 30-31, 1072 MH Amsterdam, Netherlands

  • 4- FLAMINGO5

    This bar is right at the centre of the market and offered a nice spot to people watch and soak up the atmosphere and try local beer.

    Form more information visit FLAMINGO website.

    Address: Eerste van der Helststraat 37, 1073 AC Amsterdam

  • 5- GUINESS Cafe KrullGlouglou

    This bar is perfect for a late drinks at night. The bar has a good choice of spirits and beers and staff friendly.

    Address: Sarphatipark 2, 1072 PA Amsterdam, Netherlands
